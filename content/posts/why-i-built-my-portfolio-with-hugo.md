I chose Hugo because I wanted a portfolio that is fast, easy to maintain, and focused on writing rather than application complexity.

PaperMod gives the site a strong baseline: responsive layout, RSS, tags, search support, code highlighting, reading time, and SEO-friendly metadata. That means I can spend more time documenting projects and less time maintaining infrastructure.

## What I Wanted

- A professional homepage
- Project case studies that are easy to update
- A technical blog with tags, categories, and RSS
- Fast builds and simple deployment
- Enough customization to make the site feel personal

## Why Static Works

For a portfolio, most content changes through Markdown. A static site keeps the deployment simple and avoids unnecessary runtime complexity.

## Next Steps

The most important work is not adding more site features. It is publishing useful project writeups and technical notes consistently.
